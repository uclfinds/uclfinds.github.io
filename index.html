About FINDS

UCL Financial Industry Series, or more commonly known as UCL FINDS is a student-run forum that organises high-impact conferences, debates and talks on financial topics. We were founded in December 2010 with the purpose of providing students, and professionals alike, with the opportunity to look into the latest topics within the financial industry. Attendees can not only develop a comprehensive understanding of the field but also benefit from unique networking opportunities.

Our Vision

To empower the most ambitious students at UCL with the knowledge they need for a career in the financial services industry specifically focused on technology.

Events

Some of the work we do

Supported by World Leading Financial Institutions. As a UCL-approved organisation, we endeavour to promote our university and to build and maintain strong relationships with firms within the industry. In the past, our events have met great demand from students at UCL and other top UK universities as well as from professionals within the industry. Especially our annually run Hackathon events we host alongside our sponsors. We frequently host high-impact conferences, debates and talks on financial topics with the purpose of providing students and professionals alike with the opportunity to look into the latest topics within the financial industry, and also provide exclusive networking opportunities.

How we do it

We host two major conferences each year â€“ the Financial Markets Conference and the FinTech Conference â€“ which brings in industry experts and thought leaders to discuss the changes in the financial services landscapes. This is to help you understand the latest news for use in your interviews.

We host career talks with Director-level or above executives from our partner firms (Citi, BNP Paribas, Morgan Stanley, Commerzbank ect.) to give you insight about how best to approach the job hunt and what these firms are specifically looking for when they hire. We network extensively with FinTech accelerators and incubators (Level39, Startupbootcamp, Mass Challenge, Winton Labs ect.) to keep ourselves with the latest in the startup world. This connectivity allows us to source unique opportunities for those students with a more entrepreneurial leaning.
